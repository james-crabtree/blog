HUMAN SKILLS REFLECTIONS: Helping create a supportive and inclusive work culture.

A disclaimer, I have not done weekly or fortnightly reflections on this topic like the Trello ticket has asked, instead I am doing one big reflection at the end.


I have contributed to a positive, supportive and inclusive team culture and environment in a few ways. The first way I can think of is being kind to others and supporting them. I would try and check in with the people around me, ask them how they’re doing and listen to them when they responded and provided feedback and support whenever they wanted it or needed it. While I spent a lot of the bootcamp behind most other people in terms of programming knowledge and ability, I also tried to help people out with their coding as much as I possibly could when I had the opportunity.


Another way I contributed to a positive work culture is by just generally being a good person. We are very fortunate to have a really amazing group of people on this cohort and that is because we are all kind, considerate and nice to each other. One thing I strongly believe is that humour helps ease stress and makes an environment better to be around, so I tried to provide humour wherever possible and appropriate to help lower the stress levels of the group and to help raise the vibe a little bit.
